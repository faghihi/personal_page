Official Publications


Enhancing SitOPT using Hybrid Learning Technique (Decision Tree + Situation Template )

Hossein Rajaby Faghihi, Jafar Habibi, Ehsan Shafiee
Conference Papers (Persian) 23th Conference of National Computer Society of IRAN

Abstract

Considering characteristics of intelligent environments we have proposed a hybrid method to achieve situation awareness. the method is a mixture of situation template and decision tree. one part responsible for predefined knowledge and one part reposible for environmental adaption. the results shows us an improvement in lot of cases evaluated.
